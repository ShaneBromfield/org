* Create account

#+begin_src sh
/msg NickServ identify <account> <pass>
#+end_src

* Enforce account

Avoid having his account nickname stolen.

#+begin_src sh
/nickserv set enforce on
#+end_src

* Change your pass

#+begin_src sh
/nickserv set password <new-pass>
#+end_src
